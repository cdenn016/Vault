\chapter*{Executive Summary}
\addcontentsline{toc}{chapter}{Executive Summary}

This white paper defines a gauge-covariant structured latent-variable language model and separates definitions, identities, propositions, implementation choices, and empirical hypotheses. Its proof dependency begins by fixing the scope and claim status before introducing observations, geometry, a generative joint, a recognition law, and the ELBO that connects them.

\chapter{Scope, Contribution, and Claim Status}

The manuscript's contribution is a construction of one normalized causal model and one normalized structured posterior whose inference and parameter-learning operations are evaluated against one ELBO. It does not infer predictive performance, convergence, posterior exactness, or scalability from the construction itself.
