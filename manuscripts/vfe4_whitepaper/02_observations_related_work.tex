\chapter{Language Tokens as Observations}

This chapter fixes observed tokens and causal predictive semantics before the geometric and probabilistic objects are introduced. It distinguishes target-conditioned recognition during training from the prior-based predictive distribution required for evaluation and generation.

\chapter{Relation to Variational Sequence Models}

This chapter positions the construction relative to variational sequence modeling without using that comparison as a derivation of the proposed model. The later chapters supply the normalized factors, structured posterior, and ELBO needed to make the comparison precise.
