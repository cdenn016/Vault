\chapter{Observations, Latent Variables, Structural Data, and Reference Measures}

This chapter establishes the sample-space and conditioning conventions for the probabilistic core. General statistical fields remain section-valued over \(\mathcal C\), but exact probability statements are made at one context or on a finite design \(D\), where evaluation yields a finite population product space. Observations, latent variables, structural data, and reference measures are assigned distinct types. This boundary prevents an unprovided continuum section-space measure from being assumed by notation alone.
