\chapter{Executable MAgent Crosswalk}

This chapter is reserved for a source-traceable comparison between the paper's defined objects and the active MAgent runtime. It will identify the implemented scalar, observation pathway, transports, update schedule, and closure operations under the active configuration, while keeping those implementation facts distinct from the new fixed-joint theory. The chapter does not alter application code or configuration. Its role is to make both correspondence and non-correspondence explicit enough for later verification.
