\chapter{Comparison with VFE 4.0}

This chapter provides a controlled comparison with VFE 4.0 while preserving the independence of the two documents. The comparison will align report-level categories such as geometry, observations, latent variables, generative factorization, recognition family, gauge variables, configuration tier, and executable status. Shared presentation architecture does not establish theoretical equivalence. The chapter's purpose is to state where analogous terms have different constructional roles and where direct comparison is appropriate.
