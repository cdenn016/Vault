\chapter{The PIFB2 Functional: Exact Sectors, Obstruction, and Exact Lifts}

This chapter locates the legacy PIFB2 population functional relative to the fixed-joint construction without identifying the two by terminology alone. It will separate fixed-kernel probabilistic components from live-marginal consensus energies, optional regularizers, and configuration-level lifts. The relevant obstruction is scoped to its open-family assumptions, while frozen-source, restricted-family, auxiliary-variable, structured-recognition, and configuration constructions retain their own status. This organization permits project claims to be documented without using them as authority for standard variational mathematics.
