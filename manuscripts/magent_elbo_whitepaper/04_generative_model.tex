\chapter{A Fixed Normalized Gauge-Covariant Multi-Agent Model}

This chapter defines the generative object as one fixed normalized conditional joint \(p_\theta(o,dY\mid X)\). Structural information \(X\) is declared before the model factors are specified, and no factor is permitted to read a live variational marginal. The reference construction will use normalized kernels whose transformation laws respect the chosen gauge action, including covariance congruence and the corresponding inverse congruence for precision. Its role is to provide the probability law against which recognition is assessed.
