**Previous claim.** Pointwise surjectivity of `M_ij=U_i^{-T}U_j^TΣ_j^{-1}` onto `GL(K)` proved equality between the gauge score family and a standard head with shared `W_QW_K^T`, and the RoPE identification held on raw content for general covariance.

### 5.10 Independent gradient axes, deterministic scope, and exact Fisher scaling

**Previous claim.** The displayed deterministic attention-weighted gradient simultaneously represented the canonical free energy and the deployed update; the `ε→0` adaptive self-coupling had a finite mean-only limit; one effective rate `tilde_eta=eta sigma^2` removed the isotropic Fisher factors without changing the other coefficients; and filtering/smoothing was the same distinction as canonical/surrogate.

**Counterexample or obstruction.** The two implementation seams are independent. `include_attention_entropy=True` selects the canonical entropy-augmented objective, while `False` selects the entropy-suppressed surrogate. `gradient_mode="filtering"` detaches key roles and returns each belief's own query-row derivative; `gradient_mode="smoothing"` keeps key roles live and returns the global derivative, including query-row and key-column contributions. Either gradient mode can be paired with either entropy choice. When adaptive self-coupling is active, both canonical and surrogate paths retain the same `α_i D(q_i||p_i)+R(α_i)` block with `R(α_i)=b_0α_i-c_0 log α_i`; dropping `R` changes the objective. For `q_i=N(μ_i,εI)` and fixed nondegenerate `p_i=N(μ_p,σ_p^2I)`, the full KL diverges as `ε→0`, so `α_i^*=c_0/(b_0+D_KL)→0`. Replacing the full KL by its mean-dependent part defines a different mean-only gate; it is not the adaptive-alpha limit. A fixed positive `α_i` gives a separate valid deterministic specialization.

**Revised result.** Canonical versus surrogate is controlled only by `include_attention_entropy`; filtering versus smoothing is controlled only by `gradient_mode`. The canonical path omits the through-softmax response at its row minimizer, while the surrogate retains it, and both retain `R(α_i)` whenever the adaptive gate is enabled. Filtering is the target-blind own-row belief update used by default; smoothing differentiates the global belief objective. The vocabulary cross-entropy is a separate training objective, so neither mode is a coordinate E-step on one observation-bearing functional and neither carries an EM monotonicity guarantee.

**Exact Fisher scaling.** If the isotropic Euclidean mean gradient is written as `g=λ_pδ+σ^{-2}A-L`, where `A` is the alignment contribution under the selected canonical/surrogate convention and `L` is the likelihood gradient, then the natural step is `-ησ^2g=-η(σ^2λ_pδ+A-σ^2L)`. Fisher preconditioning cancels `σ^{-2}` only in `A`; it multiplies the prior and likelihood terms by `σ^2`. No single replacement `tilde_eta=eta sigma^2` yields an unrescaled bracket containing all three original coefficients. In the deployed target-blind filter, `L` is absent and the cross-entropy update remains separate.

**Surviving scope.** The deterministic Gaussian limit at fixed `α_i`, an explicitly declared mean-only gate as a redefinition, the prior-regularization correspondence, direct message-aggregation direction, canonical envelope derivative, optional surrogate response, filtering and smoothing derivatives, state-dependent regularizer, and Gaussian Fisher formulas survive under these separate conventions.

### 5.11 Nonuniform hard-attention response

**Previous claim.** The surrogate response `-tau^{-1}Cov_beta(E,grad E)` vanished throughout the hard-attention limit, with every near-tie correction decaying as `O(tau)`.

**Counterexample or obstruction.** For two keys with `E_1-E_2=c tau` and `grad E_1-grad E_2=d`, the response converges to the finite value `-c d/[4 cosh^2(c/2)]`. Hence convergence to zero is not uniform over configurations whose energy gap shrinks with temperature.

**Revised result.** At each fixed configuration with a unique positive gap, the response vanishes as `tau→0`; in the temperature-scaled near-tie layer it can remain finite. Exact ties, fixed positive gaps, and paths approaching a tie are distinct limits.

**Surviving scope.** The fixed-gap pointwise hard-attention limit and the exact covariance-gap identity survive. The covariance and mean response discussions must retain the finite near-tie limit rather than assert uniform `O(tau)` decay.
