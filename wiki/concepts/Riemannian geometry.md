# Riemannian geometry

Riemannian geometry studies smooth manifolds equipped with a metric tensor that defines inner products on tangent spaces, yielding notions of length, angle, geodesics, parallel transport, curvature, and the exponential/logarithm maps. It is the mathematical backbone of the program's geometric machinery: the SPD covariance manifold with its affine-invariant metric, the Fisher-information / statistical manifold, natural-gradient descent, and Riemannian optimization all instantiate Riemannian-geometric structure on spaces of belief states.
