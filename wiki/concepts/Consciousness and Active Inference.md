# Consciousness and Active Inference

A research strand that proposes consciousness can be characterized within the free-energy / active-inference framework: phenomenal experience is tied to the structure of a self-modeling generative process minimizing variational free energy, with selfhood, temporal depth, and counterfactual/precision dynamics as candidate correlates of conscious processing. It bridges the hard problem and the combination problem of panpsychism with predictive-processing accounts of the brain.
