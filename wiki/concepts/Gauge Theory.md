# Gauge Theory

Gauge theory describes systems with a local symmetry: a Lie group acting fibrewise on a principal/associated bundle, where physics (or inference) is invariant under position-dependent group transformations. A connection (gauge field) defines parallel transport and its curvature measures holonomy. The VFE program reads belief dynamics as a gauge theory — local frame choices on the SPD/statistical manifold are gauge, attention transports beliefs along a connection, and curvature/holonomy encode path-dependent belief change.
