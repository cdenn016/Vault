# Belief coupling

The interaction term by which one agent's belief is pulled toward another's, formalized in the multi-agent VFE model as a gauge-transported KL coupling KL(q_i || Omega_ij q_j) between fibre-bundle sections. It generalizes the bounded-confidence / consensus interactions of opinion dynamics (Deffuant) and the product-of-experts combination of distributions (Hinton), and is the inter-agent force that drives consensus, polarization, and the emergence of meta-agents in the program.
