# Attention as Precision

The active-inference / predictive-coding interpretation of attention as the optimization of expected precision (inverse variance) on prediction errors. Rather than a separate selection mechanism, attention is the gain modulation of ascending prediction-error signals, weighting them by their estimated reliability. This grounds neural-network attention weights as precision (Fisher-information) estimates, directly connecting the program's gauge/precision-weighted attention to Friston's free-energy formulation.
