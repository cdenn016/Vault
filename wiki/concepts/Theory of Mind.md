# Theory of Mind

Theory of mind (ToM) is the cognitive capacity to attribute mental states - beliefs, desires, intentions - to other agents and to use those attributions to predict and explain behavior. In the active-inference framing, ToM is recast as one agent embedding a generative model of another agent's generative model, so that inferring another's hidden beliefs becomes part of minimizing one's own variational free energy. This nested-model view connects ToM to multi-agent VFE, communication as shared generative models, and the emergence of meta-agents.
