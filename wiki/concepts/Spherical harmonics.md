# Spherical harmonics

Spherical harmonics are the orthonormal basis functions on the 2-sphere that carry the irreducible representations of SO(3); the degree-l harmonics span the (2l+1)-dimensional irrep of angular momentum l. In SE(3)/SO(3)-equivariant networks (Tensor Field Networks, SE(3)-Transformer, 3D steerable CNNs) they appear as steerable angular filter bases, so that radial functions times spherical harmonics give rotation-equivariant convolution kernels. They link directly to group representation theory and the Clebsch-Gordan decomposition used to combine features.
