# Consciousness

Consciousness denotes subjective experience and its scientific/philosophical study, spanning the 'hard problem', candidate theories (integrated information theory, global workspace, Orch-OR), and metaphysical stances (panpsychism, realistic monism). In the gauge-theoretic VFE program it connects to self-modeling agents, free-energy accounts of awareness, and the participatory it-from-bit thesis that observers are constitutive of facts. This page is a hub pointing to the deeper theme and theory pages.
