# Objective Reduction

Objective Reduction (OR) is Penrose's proposal that quantum state-vector collapse is a real, observer-independent physical process triggered by gravity rather than by measurement or decoherence. A superposition of two mass distributions is gravitationally unstable and self-reduces on a timescale T set by E = ħ/T, where E is the gravitational self-energy of the mass difference. OR is the physical mechanism underlying the Orch OR theory of consciousness and is closely related to the Diósi-Penrose model; in this program it bears on whether observer-dependent collapse can be given an objective grounding, a question participatory realism addresses information-theoretically.
