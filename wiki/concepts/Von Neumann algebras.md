# Von Neumann algebras

A von Neumann algebra is a *-algebra of bounded operators on a Hilbert space that is closed in the weak operator topology and contains the identity (equivalently, equals its own double commutant). They are the natural setting for algebraic quantum theory and quantum statistical mechanics, supporting a type classification (I, II, III) and Tomita-Takesaki modular theory. In this program they appear via Connes-Rovelli's thermal-time hypothesis, where the algebra-state pair generates an intrinsic modular flow used to model observer/state-relative time.
