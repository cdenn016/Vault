# Belief perseverance and confirmation bias

**Belief perseverance** is persistence after the original evidence has been withdrawn or discredited. **Confirmation bias** is a family of biases in search, attention, evaluation, memory, and interpretation. The theorem-first revision [[belief-inertia-2026-07-12-theorem-first-revision]] does not derive either construct in full. It separates three narrower mechanisms that were previously conflated.

## Selective exposure

Given a fixed candidate set and a similarity-decreasing Gibbs kernel, sources closer to an agent's current belief receive more attention. This is **similarity-weighted selective exposure conditional on the kernel**. It does not derive active search, asymmetric evaluation of equally diagnostic evidence, memory distortion, or motivated reasoning. Active selection requires an explicit policy or sampling model, as in [[albarracin-2022-epistemic-communities]].

## Revision latency

At frozen optimal attention and local consensus, the gauge-VFE loss Hessian supplies prior, sensory, incoming relational, and outgoing relational stiffness. Large positive stiffness can support a controlled prediction of slower first-order revision under matched forcing and Fisher geometry. If a positive kinetic metric is separately postulated, a force-free coast or damping time can provide another candidate latency mechanism.

Neither statement licenses the global identity "precision is psychological mass." The intrinsic Fisher metric $G$, loss stiffness $H_F$, and kinetic metric $M$ are distinct. If $M=H_F$ is used for both sides of the same local oscillator equation, its modal spectrum is trivial up to scale. Any coasting or oscillation test must therefore identify kinetic and restoring quantities separately. See [[Mass as Fisher information]] and [[Hamiltonian belief dynamics]].

[[nevin-mandell-atak-1983-behavioral-momentum]] provides an empirical resistance-to-disruption construct in conditioned behavior. It motivates perturbation designs but is not evidence that opinions obey Hamiltonian mechanics or possess Fisher-information inertia.

## Explanatory perseverance

The causal-explanation mechanism documented by [[anderson1980-belief-perseverance]] requires a state that can store the explanation after the original evidence is removed. The current fast Gaussian belief state does not contain that variable. A candidate two-timescale extension could use the slow generative-model or inherited-prior states in [[participatory-it-from-bit]], but perseverance is not derived until the slow state, its update, and its coupling to the fast belief are explicitly specified.

The outgoing relational/recoil block is not such a memory state. It is contemporaneous curvature created by the source role in the current scalar objective. It cannot be described as accumulated influence, lost flexibility, or an enduring personality trait without additional dynamics.

## Adaptive prior precision

For $D_i=D_{\mathrm{KL}}(q_i\|p_i)$, the complete optimized sector is

$$
\alpha_iD_i+b_0\alpha_i-c_0\log\alpha_i,
\qquad
\alpha_i^*=\frac{c_0}{b_0+D_i}.
$$

Its envelope force coefficient is $c_0/(b_0+D_i)$. The derivative of the bare product $\alpha_i^*D_i$ is instead $b_0c_0/(b_0+D_i)^2$. These different objectives cannot be treated as the same entrenchment mechanism. A decreasing optimized coefficient is a model-specific adaptive anchor, not a general psychological law.

## Falsification requirements

A useful experiment should distinguish at least the candidate-set policy, the first-order revision metric, any kinetic metric, restoring stiffness, damping, and a slow explanatory state. Alternative oscillation or persistence mechanisms must be included: [[martins-2015-opinion-particles]] already provides inertial opinion particles, [[baumann-sokolov-tyloo-2020-second-order-consensus]] provides network resonance, and [[sampson-porter-restrepo-2025-oscillatory-opinion]] provides oscillatory group-feedback dynamics without the proposed kinetic mechanism. No empirical validation of the gauge-VFE kinetic interpretation is presently claimed.
