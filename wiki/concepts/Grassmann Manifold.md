# Grassmann Manifold

The Grassmann manifold Gr(k,n) is the space of all k-dimensional linear subspaces of an n-dimensional vector space — a compact Riemannian quotient of the Stiefel manifold by the orthogonal group O(k), and the canonical example of a [[Riemannian Quotient Manifold]]. It carries a natural invariant metric and geodesics expressible via SVD, supporting principal-angle distances and Riemannian optimization. It arises in the program through fixed-rank SPD geometry: a fixed-rank PSD matrix factors into a subspace (a Grassmann point) plus a full-rank SPD factor on that subspace, as in [[bonnabel-2009-spd-fixed-rank]].
