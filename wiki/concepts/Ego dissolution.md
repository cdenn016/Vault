# Ego dissolution

Ego dissolution is the experiential loss or weakening of the sense of a bounded self, prominently induced by psychedelics and certain meditative states. In predictive-processing / free-energy accounts it is modeled as a flattening of high-level self-model priors (relaxed precision on the self-evidencing hierarchy), so the self-boundary represented by a Markov blanket becomes less rigid. It is a key phenomenon for theories linking selfhood, precision-weighting, and the construction of subjective experience.
