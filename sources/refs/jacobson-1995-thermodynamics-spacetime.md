# Thermodynamics of Spacetime: The Einstein Equation of State

> [!info] Citation
> Ted Jacobson (1995). "Thermodynamics of spacetime: the Einstein equation of state." *Physical Review Letters* 75(7): 1260–1263. DOI: [10.1103/PhysRevLett.75.1260](https://doi.org/10.1103/PhysRevLett.75.1260). Preprint: [arXiv:gr-qc/9504004](https://arxiv.org/abs/gr-qc/9504004).

## TL;DR

Jacobson derives the full nonlinear Einstein equation as a thermodynamic *equation of state* rather than a fundamental field equation. Demanding that the Clausius relation $\delta Q = T\,\delta S$ hold for the heat flux across every local Rindler (acceleration) horizon, with $T$ the Unruh temperature and $S = A/4$ the Bekenstein–Hawking entropy proportional to horizon area, forces the Einstein equation to hold. Gravity is recast as an emergent, thermodynamic phenomenon: spacetime curvature is the macroscopic response of underlying microscopic degrees of freedom, not a primitive.

## What it establishes

- The Einstein field equations follow from local horizon thermodynamics plus the proportionality of entropy to area, for all local Rindler horizons through every point.
- Gravity is thereby "emergent" in the same sense that the ideal-gas law is emergent: a coarse-grained equation of state, not a microscopic law.
- It is the foundational move that the entanglement-equilibrium ([[jacobson-2016-entanglement-equilibrium]]) and entanglement-first-law ([[faulkner-2014-gravitation-from-entanglement]]) derivations later refine and connect to quantum information.

## Why the project cites it

Jacobson 1995 is the anchor of the entire emergent-spacetime subsection of the PIFB manuscript ([[participatory-it-from-bit]]). Its core logic — a *constraint on an information/entropy functional* (here the Clausius relation tied to area-entropy) yields the field equations of geometry — is the precise template PIFB invokes for its own thesis that physical and geometric structure emerge from the stationarity of an information functional, the [[Variational free energy]], rather than being postulated. The Unruh-temperature/area-entropy input is the physics realization of "geometry encodes information," paralleling PIFB's emergent metric from the pullback [[Fisher information metric]]. It is the headline reference for the [[Emergent spacetime and holography]] theme and the strongest external support for the [[Participatory realism (it from bit)]] reading; it sits beside [[verlinde-2011-entropic-gravity]] and [[padmanabhan-2010-thermodynamic-gravity]] as the thermodynamic-gravity triad.
