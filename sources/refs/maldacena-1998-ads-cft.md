# The Large N Limit of Superconformal Field Theories and Supergravity

> [!info] Citation
> Juan Maldacena (1998). "The large N limit of superconformal field theories and supergravity." *Advances in Theoretical and Mathematical Physics* 2(2): 231–252. DOI: [10.4310/ATMP.1998.v2.n2.a1](https://doi.org/10.4310/ATMP.1998.v2.n2.a1). Preprint: [arXiv:hep-th/9711200](https://arxiv.org/abs/hep-th/9711200).

## TL;DR

Maldacena conjectures an exact duality — the AdS/CFT correspondence — between a gravitational (string/supergravity) theory in a $(d+1)$-dimensional anti-de Sitter bulk and a conformal field theory living on its $d$-dimensional boundary. A theory *with* dynamical gravity is fully equivalent to a lower-dimensional theory *without* gravity, the sharpest realization of the holographic principle and the framework within which essentially all subsequent emergent-spacetime-from-entanglement work (RT, Van Raamsdonk, Faulkner et al., Swingle) is formulated.

## What it establishes

- A concrete, calculable holographic duality: type IIB string theory on $AdS_5 \times S^5$ is dual to $\mathcal{N}=4$ super-Yang–Mills on the four-dimensional boundary, with a precise dictionary between bulk fields and boundary operators.
- Bulk gravitational dynamics is encoded entirely in a non-gravitational boundary theory — the holographic principle made operational.
- The arena in which entanglement entropy ([[ryu-takayanagi-2006-holographic-entanglement-entropy]]) acquires a geometric (minimal-surface) meaning and in which "spacetime from entanglement" ([[vanraamsdonk-2010-entanglement-spacetime]]) becomes a derivable statement.

## Why the project cites it

AdS/CFT is the foundational correspondence behind the whole emergent-spacetime literature PIFB ([[participatory-it-from-bit]]) surveys: it is the setting in which a *geometric, gravitational* description (the bulk) is shown to be informationally equivalent to a *non-geometric* boundary description, the strongest known precedent for the claim that geometry is emergent and encodeable in lower-dimensional informational data. For the program this licenses the move PIFB attempts in miniature — recovering metric/geometric structure from the informational content of belief fibres via the pullback [[Fisher information metric]]. It is the keystone HIGH-priority node of the [[Emergent spacetime and holography]] theme and the technical backbone of the [[Participatory realism (it from bit)]] reading.
