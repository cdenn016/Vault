# The Unity of Consciousness: Binding, Integration, Dissociation

> [!info] Citation
> Cleeremans, Axel (2003). "The Unity of Consciousness: Binding, Integration, Dissociation." Oxford University Press. https://doi.org/10.1093/acprof:oso/9780198508571.001.0001

## TL;DR
An edited interdisciplinary volume gathering philosophical, psychological, and neuroscientific perspectives on the unity of consciousness — how binding, integration, and dissociation shape unified conscious experience. It is the volume in which Bayne and Chalmers's 'What is the Unity of Consciousness?' appears.

## Relevance to this research
The host volume for the Bayne-Chalmers unity-of-consciousness chapter that the PIFB manuscript draws on for the phenomenal-unity / total-phenomenal-state notion linked to the global VFE minimum.
