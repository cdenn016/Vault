# Gauge Theory Correlators from Non-Critical String Theory

> [!info] Citation
> Gubser, S. S., Klebanov, Igor R., Polyakov, Alexander M. (1998). "Gauge Theory Correlators from Non-Critical String Theory." Phys. Lett. B 428 (1998) 105-114. https://arxiv.org/abs/hep-th/9802109

## TL;DR
Independently of Witten, proposes the GKP relation realizing the gauge/gravity (AdS/CFT) duality: correlation functions of gauge-theory operators are obtained from the bulk string/supergravity action evaluated with sources set by boundary values of bulk fields.

## Relevance to this research
Co-founding paper of the AdS/CFT gauge/gravity dictionary cited by Maldacena's review, supporting the holographic, information-theoretic view of emergent spacetime in the program.
