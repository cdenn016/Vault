# A Disciplined Approach to Neural Network Hyper-Parameters (Cyclical LR)

> [!info] Citation
> Leslie N. Smith (2018). *A Disciplined Approach to Neural Network Hyper-Parameters (Cyclical LR)*. arXiv:1803.09820 (US Naval Research Laboratory Tech. Report). https://arxiv.org/abs/1803.09820

## TL;DR

A practical guide to setting learning rate, batch size, momentum, and weight decay, advocating cyclical / one-cycle learning-rate schedules and the LR-range test; companion to Smith's earlier 'Cyclical Learning Rates for Training Neural Networks' (WACV 2017, arXiv:1506.01186).

## Relevance to this research

Cyclical/one-cycle learning-rate practice; hyperparameter-tuning context.
