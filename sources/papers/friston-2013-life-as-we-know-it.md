# Life as we know it

> [!info] Citation
> Friston, Karl (2013). "Life as we know it." Journal of the Royal Society Interface. https://doi.org/10.1098/rsif.2013.0475

## TL;DR
Derives the free-energy principle from first principles for any ergodic random dynamical system possessing a Markov blanket. Shows that the existence of a Markov blanket separating internal from external states implies that internal states appear to infer (and act to minimize free energy over) external states, grounding self-organization and life-like behaviour in nonequilibrium steady-state dynamics.

## Relevance to this research
Establishes the Markov-blanket / nonequilibrium-steady-state derivation of the free-energy principle that underpins the program's treatment of agents as self-organizing inference systems and the emergence of hierarchical meta-agents.
