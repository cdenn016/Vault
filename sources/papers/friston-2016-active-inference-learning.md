# Active Inference and Learning

> [!info] Citation
> Karl Friston, Thomas FitzGerald, Francesco Rigoli, Philipp Schwartenbeck, John O'Doherty, Giovanni Pezzulo (2016). *Active Inference and Learning*. Neuroscience & Biobehavioral Reviews 68:862-879 (2016). https://doi.org/10.1016/j.neubiorev.2016.06.022

## TL;DR

Casts learning as the slow minimization of variational free energy over model parameters, complementing the fast inference (perception) and action (control) updates of active inference, so that perception, action, and learning are one gradient flow on free energy at different timescales.

## Relevance to this research

Active-inference treatment of learning as free-energy minimization over time; complements the present Friston active-inference notes.
