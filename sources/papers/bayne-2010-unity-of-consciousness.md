# The Unity of Consciousness

> [!info] Citation
> Bayne, Tim (2010). "The Unity of Consciousness." Oxford University Press. https://doi.org/10.1093/acprof:oso/9780199215386.001.0001

## TL;DR
Bayne's monograph defends the 'unity thesis': that a subject's conscious states at a time are necessarily subsumed into a single, phenomenally unified total experience. It develops the mereological 'phenomenal unity' framework and addresses apparent counterexamples (split-brain, dissociative, and pathological cases).

## Relevance to this research
Cited by the Tononi 2004 IIT note as the philosophical treatment of phenomenal unity, which IIT operationalizes via integrated information (Phi); relevant to the participatory/consciousness strand of the multi-agent program.
