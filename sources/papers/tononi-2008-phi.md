# Consciousness as Integrated Information: a Provisional Manifesto

> [!info] Citation
> Tononi, Giulio (2008). "Consciousness as Integrated Information: a Provisional Manifesto." The Biological Bulletin 215(3): 216-242. https://doi.org/10.2307/25470707

## TL;DR
Tononi's manifesto formulation of Integrated Information Theory (IIT), introducing phi as a scalar measure of the integrated information generated by a system above and beyond its parts. Consciousness is identified with a system's capacity to integrate information, quantified by phi. This is the canonical pre-IIT-3.0 statement of the phi quantity.

## Relevance to this research
IIT's phi as an integration-of-information measure is a participatory/consciousness reference point for the program's information-theoretic and multi-agent integration constructs; cited by the global-neuronal-workspace source as a rival/companion theory of consciousness.
