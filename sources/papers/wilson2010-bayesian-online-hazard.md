# Bayesian Online Learning of the Hazard Rate in Change-Point Problems

> [!info] Citation
> Wilson, Robert C., Nassar, Matthew R., Gold, Joshua I. (2010). "Bayesian Online Learning of the Hazard Rate in Change-Point Problems." Neural Computation. https://doi.org/10.1162/NECO_a_00007

## TL;DR
Develops a Bayesian online change-point detection algorithm that simultaneously infers the generative state and learns the hazard rate (probability of a change point) from data, generalizing fixed-hazard change-point inference. Provides a reduced, computationally tractable approximation to the full run-length posterior with adaptive learning rates.

## Relevance to this research
Companion to Nassar et al. 2010; the hazard-rate inference machinery is structurally analogous to the VFE program's hyperprior-driven adaptive belief-updating and learning-rate modulation, and grounds approximate-Bayesian belief dynamics empirically.
