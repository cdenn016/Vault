# A Cognitive Theory of Consciousness

> [!info] Citation
> Baars, Bernard J. (1989). "A Cognitive Theory of Consciousness." Cambridge University Press.

## TL;DR
Baars's foundational statement of Global Workspace Theory: consciousness functions as a 'global workspace' that broadcasts information to a distributed set of specialized unconscious processors, enabling flexible coordination. The workspace metaphor underlies later neuronal-workspace (Dehaene) models.

## Relevance to this research
Original source of Global Workspace Theory, the cognitive-architecture ancestor of the global-neuronal-workspace model used as a consciousness reference for the participatory and multi-agent strands of the program.
