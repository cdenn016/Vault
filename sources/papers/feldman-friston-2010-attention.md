# Attention, uncertainty, and free-energy

> [!info] Citation
> Feldman, Harriet, Friston, Karl (2010). "Attention, uncertainty, and free-energy." Frontiers in Human Neuroscience. https://doi.org/10.3389/fnhum.2010.00215

## TL;DR
Recasts attention within the free-energy / predictive-coding framework as the optimization of precision (inverse variance) on prediction errors. Attention is the expected precision of sensory channels, implemented as gain control on prediction-error units; the paper links psychophysical attention effects (e.g. Posner cueing) to Bayes-optimal precision-weighting.

## Relevance to this research
Directly grounds the program's precision-weighted attention story: attention as precision/Fisher-information weighting of prediction errors is the neuroscientific antecedent of the VFE transformer's precision-weighted attention mechanism.
