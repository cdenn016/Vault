# Word and Object

> [!info] Citation
> Quine, W. V. O. (1960). "Word and Object." MIT Press. https://mitpress.mit.edu/9780262518314/word-and-object/

## TL;DR
Quine's central work developing the indeterminacy of translation, the inscrutability of reference, and a thoroughgoing naturalized, behaviorist account of language and meaning. It extends the holism of 'Two Dogmas' into a full theory in which meaning is fixed only relative to a translation manual and ontology is relative to a background language.

## Relevance to this research
Extends Quine's holistic web-of-belief epistemology into semantics and ontology, reinforcing the program's structural-realist, observer-relative reading of meaning and reference relevant to participatory-realism foundations.
