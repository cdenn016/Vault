# Generalised free energy and active inference

> [!info] Citation
> Parr, Thomas, Friston, Karl J. (2019). "Generalised free energy and active inference." Biological Cybernetics 113(5-6), 495-513. https://doi.org/10.1007/s00422-019-00805-w

## TL;DR
Parr and Friston introduce a generalised free energy functional for active inference that unifies expected free energy (epistemic + pragmatic value) with risk- and ambiguity-based decompositions under partial observability. It clarifies how exploratory and exploitative behavior emerge from a single variational objective over policies.

## Relevance to this research
Provides the generalised/expected-free-energy machinery for policy selection that underpins the active-inference reading of the VFE program and the multi-agent model's belief-and-action dynamics.
