# Critical Phenomena in Complex Networks

> [!info] Citation
> Dorogovtsev, Sergey N., Goltsev, Alexander V., Mendes, José F. F. (2008). "Critical Phenomena in Complex Networks." Reviews of Modern Physics. https://doi.org/10.1103/RevModPhys.80.1275

## TL;DR
A comprehensive review of cooperative phenomena and phase transitions on complex networks — the Ising and Potts models, percolation, epidemic spreading, and synchronization on scale-free and small-world graphs. It shows how heterogeneous degree distributions alter critical points and exponents, often suppressing or qualitatively changing transitions relative to regular lattices.

## Relevance to this research
Supplies the canonical reference for how network topology reshapes phase transitions and critical phenomena — directly relevant to collective inference, belief synchronization, and opinion dynamics on agent-interaction graphs in the multi-agent and social-physics arms of the program.
