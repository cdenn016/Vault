# Mathematical Foundations of Quantum Mechanics

> [!info] Citation
> von Neumann, John (1932). "Mathematical Foundations of Quantum Mechanics." Springer (Berlin); Princeton University Press (English translation, 1955). https://press.princeton.edu/books/hardcover/9780691178561/mathematical-foundations-of-quantum-mechanics

## TL;DR
The foundational treatise placing quantum mechanics on a rigorous Hilbert-space footing: states as vectors, observables as self-adjoint operators, the projection postulate / measurement (von Neumann) chain, and the density-matrix formalism with the von Neumann entropy. Introduces the distinction between unitary evolution and the measurement-induced collapse.

## Relevance to this research
Source of the von Neumann measurement model, density matrices, and von Neumann entropy that underpin the program's participatory / relational-QM and meta-entropy threads.
