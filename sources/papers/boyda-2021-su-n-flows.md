# Sampling Using SU(N) Gauge Equivariant Flows

> [!info] Citation
> Boyda, Denis, Kanwar, Gurtej, Racaniere, Sebastien, Rezende, Danilo Jimenez, Albergo, Michael S., Cranmer, Kyle, Hackett, Daniel C., Shanahan, Phiala E. (2021). "Sampling Using SU(N) Gauge Equivariant Flows." Physical Review D. https://arxiv.org/abs/2008.05456

## TL;DR
Extends gauge-equivariant normalizing flows from U(1) to non-abelian SU(N) gauge theories, constructing flows on SU(N)-valued link variables via equivariant transformations on the matrix conjugacy classes/eigenvalues. Enables exact, symmetry-respecting sampling for non-abelian lattice gauge theory, a key step toward flow-based lattice QCD.

## Relevance to this research
Non-abelian extension of gauge-equivariant generative modeling, directly informing the program's handling of matrix-valued (SU(N)/GL(K)) gauge frames and symmetry-respecting learning over Lie-group-valued variables.
