# The entropic brain - revisited

> [!info] Citation
> Carhart-Harris, Robin L. (2018). "The entropic brain - revisited." Neuropharmacology, 142:167-178. https://doi.org/10.1016/j.neuropharm.2018.03.010

## TL;DR
An updated formulation of the entropic brain hypothesis, refining the claim that the richness of conscious states tracks the entropy (criticality) of spontaneous brain dynamics. It incorporates the free-energy principle and predictive-coding machinery, framing psychedelics as agents that increase neural entropy by relaxing high-level priors.

## Relevance to this research
The entropic-brain-revisited synthesis directly links neural entropy and criticality to variational free-energy minimization and precision (prior) weighting, the same machinery formalized in the VFE program; it underpins the REBUS model the program draws on.
