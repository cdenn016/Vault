# Anti De Sitter Space And Holography

> [!info] Citation
> Witten, Edward (1998). "Anti De Sitter Space And Holography." Adv. Theor. Math. Phys. 2 (1998) 253-291. https://arxiv.org/abs/hep-th/9802150

## TL;DR
Provides the precise dictionary for the AdS/CFT correspondence: boundary CFT correlation functions are computed from bulk supergravity/string partition functions with prescribed boundary conditions for the dual fields. Establishes the operational holographic mapping between bulk fields and boundary operators.

## Relevance to this research
One of the founding AdS/CFT papers (with Maldacena and Gubser-Klebanov-Polyakov) underpinning the emergent-spacetime-from-information theme central to the participatory it-from-bit program.
