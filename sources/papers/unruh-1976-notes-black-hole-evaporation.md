# Notes on Black-Hole Evaporation

> [!info] Citation
> Unruh, W. G. (1976). "Notes on Black-Hole Evaporation." Physical Review D. https://doi.org/10.1103/PhysRevD.14.870

## TL;DR
Unruh's foundational paper showing that a uniformly accelerated observer in the Minkowski vacuum perceives a thermal bath of particles at the Unruh temperature proportional to the acceleration. It clarifies the observer-dependence of the particle concept and links acceleration, horizons, and thermodynamics.

## Relevance to this research
The Unruh temperature is a key ingredient in Verlinde's entropic-gravity derivation and exemplifies observer/frame-dependent facts central to the participatory-realism cluster of the program.
