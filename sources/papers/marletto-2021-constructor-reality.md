# The Science of Can and Can't: A Physicist's Journey through the Land of Counterfactuals

> [!info] Citation
> Marletto, Chiara (2021). "The Science of Can and Can't: A Physicist's Journey through the Land of Counterfactuals." Allen Lane / Viking.

## TL;DR
Marletto's book-length exposition of constructor theory, the Deutsch-Marletto program that recasts fundamental physics in terms of which physical transformations (tasks) are possible or impossible and why, rather than in terms of initial conditions plus dynamical laws. Counterfactual statements about possibility are treated as primary, exact physical facts.

## Relevance to this research
Constructor theory's information-as-fundamental, possibility/impossibility-based reformulation of physics is a natural reference for the participatory it-from-bit (PIFB) strand, which treats information and constraints as ontologically primary.
