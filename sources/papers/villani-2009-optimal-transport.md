# Optimal Transport: Old and New

> [!info] Citation
> Villani, C. (2009). "Optimal Transport: Old and New." Grundlehren der mathematischen Wissenschaften, vol. 338, Springer. https://doi.org/10.1007/978-3-540-71050-9

## TL;DR
Villani's comprehensive monograph on optimal transport, surveying the Monge-Kantorovich problem, Wasserstein distances and geometry, Kantorovich duality, displacement convexity, and the deep links between optimal transport, Ricci curvature, and gradient flows on the space of probability measures. It is the standard reference establishing optimal transport as a geometry on distributions.

## Relevance to this research
Supplies the rigorous geometric foundation behind the Wasserstein/optimal-transport tools the program uses to compare belief distributions and motivate geometry-aware affinities; cited as the canonical OT reference alongside the Sinkhorn computation of Cuturi.
