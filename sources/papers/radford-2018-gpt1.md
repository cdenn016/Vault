# Improving Language Understanding by Generative Pre-Training

> [!info] Citation
> Radford, A., Narasimhan, K., Salimans, T., Sutskever, I. (2018). "Improving Language Understanding by Generative Pre-Training." OpenAI Technical Report. https://cdn.openai.com/research-covers/language-unsupervised/language_understanding_paper.pdf

## TL;DR
Introduces GPT (GPT-1): a transformer decoder pretrained with an unsupervised generative language-modeling objective and then fine-tuned discriminatively on downstream tasks. Establishes the generative-pretraining-then-fine-tuning paradigm that GPT-2 and successors scaled up.

## Relevance to this research
The first GPT, defining the autoregressive transformer-decoder pretraining paradigm that the VFE transformer builds on and contrasts against.
