# The Hardware Lottery

> [!info] Citation
> Sara Hooker (2020). *The Hardware Lottery*. arXiv:2009.06489; Communications of the ACM 64(12):58-65 (2021). https://arxiv.org/abs/2009.06489

## TL;DR

Argues that which research ideas succeed is partly a 'hardware lottery' — ideas win when they fit the available hardware and software, not only on intrinsic merit — with implications for which directions get explored.

## Relevance to this research

Essay on how hardware shapes which research ideas win; meta-science context for the philosophy panel.
