# Theory of Phase Transitions: Rigorous Results

> [!info] Citation
> Sinai, Ya. G. (1982). "Theory of Phase Transitions: Rigorous Results." Pergamon Press (International Series in Natural Philosophy, vol. 108). https://www.google.com/books/edition/Theory_of_Phase_Transitions/_pY-AAAAIAAJ

## TL;DR
Sinai's monograph develops the rigorous mathematical theory of phase transitions in statistical mechanics, including renormalization-group ideas, cluster expansions, and the geometry of Gibbs states. It is a foundational reference for the rigorous (probabilistic) side of the renormalization group.

## Relevance to this research
Cited by the Jona-Lasinio note connecting the renormalization group to probability theory; grounds the RG-flow-of-beliefs analogy used in the multi-agent VFE program's coarse-graining and emergent-meta-agent picture.
