# Spin Entanglement Witness for Quantum Gravity

> [!info] Citation
> Bose, Sougato, Mazumdar, Anupam, Morley, Gavin W., Ulbricht, Hendrik, Toros, Marko, Paternostro, Mauro, Geraci, Andrew A., Barker, Peter F., Kim, M. S., Milburn, Gerard (2017). "Spin Entanglement Witness for Quantum Gravity." Physical Review Letters 119, 240401. https://arxiv.org/abs/1707.06050

## TL;DR
Proposes a tabletop experiment in which two massive particles in spatial superposition become entangled solely through their mutual gravitational interaction. Observing such gravity-induced entanglement would witness that gravity is non-classical, since a local classical (LOCC) channel cannot create entanglement.

## Relevance to this research
A concrete proposal to probe whether gravity is quantum via entanglement generation, connecting macroscopic quantum coherence to information-theoretic witnesses; relevant to the participatory quantum-foundations strand on information, gravity, and observer-dependent reality.
