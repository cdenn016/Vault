# A Differential Equation for Modeling Nesterov's Accelerated Gradient Method: Theory and Insights

> [!info] Citation
> Su, Weijie, Boyd, Stephen, Candès, Emmanuel J. (2016). "A Differential Equation for Modeling Nesterov's Accelerated Gradient Method: Theory and Insights." Journal of Machine Learning Research 17(153). https://arxiv.org/abs/1503.01243

## TL;DR
Derives a second-order ordinary differential equation as the continuous-time limit of Nesterov's accelerated gradient method, with a vanishing 3/t damping term. The ODE makes the method's oscillatory, accelerated convergence transparent and yields a Lyapunov-function analysis and new restarting schemes.

## Relevance to this research
An originating work for the continuous-time / dynamical-systems view of accelerated optimization that the Diakonikolas-Jordan Hamiltonian momentum framework generalizes, linking optimizer dynamics to the Hamiltonian and information-geometric structures studied in the program.
