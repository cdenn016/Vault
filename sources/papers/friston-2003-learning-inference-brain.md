# Learning and Inference in the Brain

> [!info] Citation
> Friston, Karl (2003). "Learning and Inference in the Brain." Neural Networks 16(9):1325-1352. https://doi.org/10.1016/j.neunet.2003.06.005

## TL;DR
Casts cortical learning and perception as empirical-Bayes inference in a hierarchical generative model, with prediction-error minimization implemented by recurrent message passing between cortical levels. An early statement of the free-energy / predictive-coding account of brain function, linking expectation-maximization and hierarchical Bayesian inference to neuronal dynamics.

## Relevance to this research
An early foundational predictive-coding/free-energy paper underlying the VFE program's claim that hierarchical variational inference is the right model of cortical computation and, by analogy, of layered attention. Cited by friston2008hierarchical.
