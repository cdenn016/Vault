# Edge modes of gravity. Part I: Corner potentials and charges

> [!info] Citation
> Freidel, Laurent, Geiller, Marc, Pranzetti, Daniele (2020). "Edge modes of gravity. Part I: Corner potentials and charges." Journal of High Energy Physics (JHEP) 2020(11):026. https://arxiv.org/abs/2006.12527

## TL;DR
Develops a systematic Hamiltonian and covariant phase-space treatment of edge modes and corner symmetry charges in gravity, identifying the corner symmetry algebra that organizes boundary degrees of freedom. Establishes how local subsystems acquire surface charges from the boundary symplectic structure.

## Relevance to this research
Extends the Donnelly-Freidel local-subsystems / edge-mode program that underpins the gauge-theoretic treatment of subsystem boundaries, surface charges, and shared gauge frames in the multi-agent VFE model.
