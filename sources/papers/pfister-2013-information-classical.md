# An information-theoretic principle implies that any discrete physical theory is classical

> [!info] Citation
> Pfister, Corsin, Wehner, Stephanie (2013). "An information-theoretic principle implies that any discrete physical theory is classical." Nature Communications. https://doi.org/10.1038/ncomms2821

## TL;DR
Pfister and Wehner formulate an information-theoretic principle and show that any discrete physical theory satisfying it must be classical, sharpening the boundary between classical and quantum/post-quantum theories within generalized probabilistic theories. The work is part of the program deriving physical structure from information-theoretic axioms.

## Relevance to this research
