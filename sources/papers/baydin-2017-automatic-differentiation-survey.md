# Automatic Differentiation in Machine Learning: a Survey

> [!info] Citation
> Atilim Gunes Baydin, Barak A. Pearlmutter, Alexey Andreyevich Radul, Jeffrey Mark Siskind (2018). *Automatic Differentiation in Machine Learning: a Survey*. JMLR 18(153):1-43, 2018; arXiv:1502.05767. https://arxiv.org/abs/1502.05767

## TL;DR

A survey distinguishing automatic differentiation from numerical and symbolic differentiation, covering forward and reverse modes, the dual-number and tape implementations, and AD's role in machine learning (where reverse-mode AD is backpropagation).

## Relevance to this research

AD survey; the backprop machinery the model is permitted to use for VFE-gradient computation.
