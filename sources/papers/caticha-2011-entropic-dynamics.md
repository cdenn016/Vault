# Entropic Dynamics, Time and Quantum Theory

> [!info] Citation
> Caticha, Ariel (2011). "Entropic Dynamics, Time and Quantum Theory." Journal of Physics A: Mathematical and Theoretical. https://arxiv.org/abs/1005.2357

## TL;DR
An early formulation of entropic dynamics in which quantum theory is derived from the entropic (maximum-entropy) inference of a probability distribution undergoing constrained diffusion, with an emergent notion of time and a deduced Schrodinger equation. Precursor to Caticha's later (2019) entropic-dynamics-of-QM syntheses.

## Relevance to this research
Establishes the information-geometric / maximum-entropy route to quantum dynamics that the program leans on for an inference-first reading of physics and the Born rule.
