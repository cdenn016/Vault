# Answering Schrödinger's question: A free-energy formulation

> [!info] Citation
> Ramstead, Maxwell J. D., Badcock, Paul B., Friston, Karl J. (2018). "Answering Schrödinger's question: A free-energy formulation." Physics of Life Reviews. https://doi.org/10.1016/j.plrev.2017.09.001

## TL;DR
Reframes Schrödinger's 'What is life?' through the free-energy principle, arguing that self-organizing biological systems persist by minimizing variational free energy and thereby resisting dispersion to equilibrium. Develops a multi-scale, nested-Markov-blanket account in which the same free-energy imperative operates from molecules to ecosystems.

## Relevance to this research
A canonical free-energy-principle paper that grounds the active-inference and Markov-blanket framing reused across the multi-agent VFE model, connecting variational free energy to self-organization and hierarchical (nested-blanket) emergence.
