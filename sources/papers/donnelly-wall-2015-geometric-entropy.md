# Entanglement entropy of electromagnetic edge modes

> [!info] Citation
> Donnelly, William, Wall, Aron C. (2015). "Entanglement entropy of electromagnetic edge modes." Physical Review Letters 114, 111603. https://arxiv.org/abs/1412.1895

## TL;DR
Shows that the entanglement entropy of a gauge field across a surface receives a distinct contribution from edge modes — degrees of freedom localized on the entangling surface — resolving ambiguities in defining geometric (entanglement) entropy for gauge theories. The edge-mode contribution accounts for the contact term in the entropy and connects to the black-hole entropy puzzle.

## Relevance to this research
Foundational result on edge modes and geometric entropy for gauge fields, supporting the surface-charge / local-subsystem structure used in the program's gauge-theoretic account of subsystem boundaries and information sharing between agents.
