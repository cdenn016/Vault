# Quantum Theory from First Principles: An Informational Approach

> [!info] Citation
> Chiribella, Giulio, D'Ariano, Giacomo Mauro, Perinotti, Paolo (2017). "Quantum Theory from First Principles: An Informational Approach." Cambridge University Press. https://doi.org/10.1017/9781107338340

## TL;DR
A book-length development of the informational reconstruction of quantum theory, deriving the Hilbert-space formalism from operational axioms (causality, purification, local distinguishability, ideal compression, atomicity of composition). The purification postulate is the distinctively quantum axiom singling out quantum theory among general probabilistic theories.

## Relevance to this research
Anchors the it-from-bit / participatory thesis that quantum theory is reconstructible from information-processing principles, supporting the program's information-first treatment of physical law.
